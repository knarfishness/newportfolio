About Frank

My story started in Virginia. After graduating in 2010, I decided it was time for a change of scenery, and relocated to Michigan.

I'm a PHP / JavaScript developer by day, and a hardware tinkerer by night. I like to find ways to make these skills go together, but I can be happy even when they don't.

I live in Redford, MI in a cozy little 50's house with my soon-to-be wife and our 3 cats, 6 fish and countless plants. When I'm not looking into new designs for homebuilt 3D printers, I'm on the hunt for any new technologies that can either make my life easier or more interesting.
